Nervozni Postar Fan Webpage

Nervozni Postar (English: Nervous Postman) is a Bosnian folk rock band, formed in 1985 by Fadil sabovic. The band is known for its combination of hard rock music and folk music. The current band members are Fadil Sabovic, Nusret Dolicanin, Dragan Durdelija, Sinisa Cekic, Dusan Simic, Goran Miladenovic and Dusan Kolarevic.
