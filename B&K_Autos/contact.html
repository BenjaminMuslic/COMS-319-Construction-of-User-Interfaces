About

B&K Autos was founded in March 12th, 2023, by visionary entrepreneurs, Benjamin Muslic and Kareem Eljaam. This website was created as a midterm project for the course SE/ComS319.


Two colleagues with an idea

Both Muslic and Eljaam are working towards getting their degrees in Computer Engineering from Iowa State University, where they honed their technical expertise. After working for leading tech companies, they were inspired to pursue their passion for automobiles and establish their own business. Drawing on their childhood love for cars, they leveraged their technical know-how to create a cutting-edge website and streamlined operations for their dealership. Driven by a commitment to excellence, Muslic and Eljaam are dedicated to providing their valued customers with unparalleled service and support for all of their driving needs.


Our Promise

B&K Autos is committed to providing our valued customers with exceptional service and premium quality vehicles. Our top priority is ensuring your satisfaction, and we take pride in delivering an unparalleled car-buying experience. We open our doors with enthusiasm, ready to provide you with personalized service and expert advice on selecting the perfect vehicle for your lifestyle and budget. At B&K, we stand behind our products and services. We offer generous warranties and stand by our policies to ensure that every customer feels confident in their purchase. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the sale, and we are always available to assist you with any future needs or concerns. Choose B&K Autos for a superior car-buying experience, where customer satisfaction is our top priority.
